ABOUT ME

I am Anastasiya Alshevskaya and my passion is art in any form of its existence. My paintings are my self-expression. I hope those who look at my works will be able to find their own source of inspiration.
A new story starts when my brushes touch a white canvas with a color. I am inspired by the world, people, nature, architecture that surround us. I always search for something special in ordinary things and I try to show it in my works.
If my art speaks to you and you would like to make an order, go ahead and reach out to me on via e-mail or phone.
